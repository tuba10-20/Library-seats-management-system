About Us

A place built for curious minds

The RIT Central Library is one of the finest institutional libraries in Western Maharashtra, blending modern architecture with a relaxed, reader-friendly environment. It offers a vast collection of 1,45,118+ volumes, 37,773+ titles, 44,515+ reference books, and 38,572+ e-books across engineering, management, and allied fields. The library also supports competitive exam preparation with resources for GATE, TOEFL, MPSC, and more.

With a total investment of ₹5.84 crores, the library subscribes to 167+ print journals, IEEE ASPP, ScienceDirect, DELNET, and other premium e-journal databases, with an annual subscription investment exceeding ₹20.73 lakhs. Tools like Crossref-Turnitin, Grammarly and Typeset further enhance academic and research quality. Our commitment to excellence has earned us accolades such as the Best Knowledge Resource Center Award (2021) and the Best Library Award (2019) from ISTE, New Delhi.
